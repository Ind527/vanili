About Nusantara Vanilla Export

Professional Indonesian vanilla export company dedicated to supplying premium quality vanilla beans to international markets with reliable service and comprehensive support.

Company Overview

Nusantara Vanilla Export is a professional Indonesian vanilla export company focused on supplying premium quality vanilla beans to international buyers, importers, and manufacturers worldwide. Our operations are strategically based in Kabupaten Tangerang, Indonesia, providing optimal access to Indonesia's prime vanilla-growing regions.

We specialize in Indonesian vanilla bean export, working directly with farmers across Java, Bali, Sulawesi, and Papua to ensure consistent quality and reliable supply for our international business partners. Our approach emphasizes quality-oriented sourcing, careful handling and processing, and comprehensive export documentation support.
