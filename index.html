Professional Indonesian Vanilla Export

Nusantara Vanilla Export specializes in supplying premium Indonesian vanilla beans to international markets. We work directly with farmers across Indonesia's prime vanilla-growing regions to ensure consistent quality and reliable supply for our global business partners.

Our export operations are based in Kabupaten Tangerang, Indonesia, strategically positioned to serve international buyers with efficient logistics and comprehensive documentation support.
