# Indonesian Vanilla Export Research

## Quality Standards & Specifications

### Grade Classifications
**Grade A (Gourmet/Premium Grade)**
- Moisture Content: 25% - 35%
- Vanillin Content: 1.8% - 2.5% (can reach 2-3.5% for Sumatra beans)
- Length: 15 cm and above (premium beans 17-25 cm)
- Appearance: Plump, oily, flexible, dark brown to black
- Surface: Glossy, shiny with oily texture
- Application: High-end cuisine, gourmet retail, premium extracts

**Grade B (Extraction/Industrial Grade)**
- Moisture Content: 15% - 25%
- Vanillin Content: 1.2% - 1.8%
- Length: 10 cm - 15 cm
- Appearance: Drier, may have some blemishes, reddish-brown to black
- Surface: Less plump, slightly oily
- Application: Large-scale extracts, food & beverage manufacturing

### Indonesian Vanilla Origins
- **Java**: Traditional Bourbon-type vanilla, deep color
- **Bali**: Premium curing facilities, sun-dried processing
- **Sulawesi**: High-quality beans with consistent specifications
- **Papua**: Large bean sizes (17cm+), high vanillin content
- **Sumatra**: Exceptional vanillin content (2.5-3.5%)

### Processing Methods
- **Traditional Curing**: Multi-month sun-drying and sweating process
- **Quality Control**: Hand-sorted and graded by experts
- **Packaging**: Vacuum-sealed in food-grade bags, export-grade cartons
- **Preservation**: Temperature-sensitive shipping, protection from air exposure

## Export Documentation Requirements

### Essential Documents
- **Phytosanitary Certificate**: Required for plant product exports
- **Certificate of Origin**: Verifies Indonesian origin
- **Export Declaration**: Indonesian customs documentation
- **Packaging List & Commercial Invoice**: Standard trade documents
- **Quality Certification (CoA)**: Laboratory testing results
- **FDA Registration**: Required for US market access
- **GACC Approval**: Required for China market access

### Incoterms Support
- **FOB (Free On Board)**: Buyer arranges main carriage
- **CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight)**: Seller arranges to destination port
- **EXW (Ex Works)**: Buyer collects from supplier premises

## Logistics & Shipping

### Packaging Standards
- **Vacuum-sealed bags**: 1kg, 5kg increments or custom requirements
- **Export-grade cartons**: Sturdy protection during transit
- **Bubble wrap padding**: Prevents physical damage
- **Food-grade materials**: Compliance with international standards
- **Eco-friendly options**: Biodegradable/recyclable materials available

### Shipping Methods
- **Air Freight**: Premium speed for urgent orders
- **Sea Freight**: Economical for large quantities ($1.50/kg typical)
- **Temperature-sensitive handling**: Maintains optimal conditions
- **International courier**: DHL, FedEx for samples

### Lead Times
- **Sample processing**: 2-3 days
- **Order preparation**: 7-14 days depending on volume
- **Documentation**: 3-5 days for export papers
- **Shipping duration**: Varies by destination and method
