Export & Logistics

Comprehensive export documentation, flexible shipping options, and professional logistics support for seamless international vanilla bean delivery.

Incoterms & Shipping Options

We offer flexible Incoterms options to meet your logistics requirements and budget preferences for international vanilla bean shipments.

FOB

Free On Board

FOB terms give you control over the main carriage while we handle export clearance and loading. Ideal for buyers with established freight forwarding relationships.

Export clearance and documentation
Loading onto vessel at port of departure
Risk transfers at port of loading
Buyer arranges main transportation

Best For:

Experienced importers with preferred shipping lines, bulk orders, and established logistics networks.

CIF

Cost, Insurance, Freight

CIF terms provide complete shipping service including insurance and freight to your destination port. Simplified logistics with single-point coordination.

All FOB services included
Main sea freight arranged by seller
Marine insurance coverage
Delivery to destination port

Best For:

Buyers seeking simplified logistics, new importers, and those preferring single-point coordination for shipping.
